Our Story

A security & facility partner built on discipline

Roushni Security and Facility Management (RSFM) is emerging as one of the leading security, cleaning and manpower solutions companies in Bangalore, India. We focus on Security Services, Night Patrolling, Facility Management, Payroll Outsourcing, Payroll Compliance and Staff Outsourcing — positioning RSFM as a professionally managed security, housekeeping and manpower service provider.

Strong workforces of well-trained security, housekeeping and pantry personnel render services round the clock towards one common objective: protecting assets, property and life.

Training & Quality Process

PSARA-Compliant Training, Start to Finish

RSFM policy mandates that every employee undertake a comprehensive training course before deployment, fulfilling PSARA Act requirements. Beyond basic guarding skills, staff are taught First Aid, fire fighting and self-defense, with training continuing progressively throughout their careers.

Basic Training

Foundational guarding skills, First Aid, fire fighting and self-defense before any deployment.

Refresher Training

Ongoing sessions that keep skills current and reinforce discipline standards.

Continuous Educational & Skill Development

Progressive programs designed around evolving client and site requirements.

Management Development Programs

Leadership and supervisory training for inspectors, supervisors and vigilance officers.

On-the-Job Training at Client Premises

Site-specific orientation so every guard understands the post they're protecting.

Supervision

A strong Operations team of Managers, Security Officers and Patrolling Supervisors — equipped with radio communications and mobile phones — carries out checks on all assignments and guards on a 24x7 basis.

Post & Site Instructions

Before any assignment, our Operations team visits the site to analyse requirements and design a setup. A detailed Charter of Duties, client expectations and emergency procedures are documented in consultation with the client.

Client Relations & Feedback

Acquiring, winning, retaining and growing — customer care has always been an RSFM tradition. We run a regular client feedback system to guide corrective action and continuous improvement.
